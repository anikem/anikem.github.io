--- layout: single author_profile: true classes: wide --- I am a senior research scientist in the Perceptual Reasoning and Interaction Research (PRIOR) team at the Allen Institute for Artificial Intelligence (AI2) in sunny Seattle. Prior to AI2, I spent five years at Microsoft Bing, building large scale machine learning systems in the Image & Video Search Relevance team.

I grew up in Pune, India where I obtained by Bachelors in Engineering at the College Of Engineering Pune (COEP). I got my Ph.D. at the University of Maryand, College Park under the supervision of Prof. Larry S. Davis.

I am interested in research problems at the intersection of vision and language including collaboration via communication and learning from interaction.
